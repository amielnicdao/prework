Rancid: The Greatest Punk Band of the '90s

Biography

Founded by former members of the band Operation Ivy, Rancid was formed in 1991 in Berkeley California. One of the best bands that retained its underground musical roots, Rancid has been credited for reviving mainstream interest in punk rock in the United States. The band has had two lineups since its conception. Currently, their line up consists of Tim Armstrong (guitars and vocals), Matt Freeman (bass and vocals), and Lars Frederiksen (guitar and vocals). Brett Reed was the only one who left the band and was replaced by Branden Steineckert (drums).

For more information, visit their Wikipedia page.
